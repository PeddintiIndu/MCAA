Lord Shiva, also known as Mahadeva, is one of the principal deities of Hinduism and a member of the Trimurti, along with Brahma and Vishnu. He is known as the destroyer and transformer of the universe,but also as a benevolent god of mercy and kindness. Shiva is often depicted as an ascetic yogi, meditating on Mount Kailash, and is also the god of destruction, dance, and arts. Lord Shiva is special in Hinduism for several reasons.
