About dccn lab 

A Data Communication and Computer Networks (DCCN) lab is a learning environment designed to provide students 
with practical experience in the field of data communication and computer networking. These labs often include hands-on 
experiments, simulations, and the use of network analysis tools to help students understand key concepts and technologies.
