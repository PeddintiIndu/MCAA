About Dccn 

DCCN, or Data Communication and Computer Networks, is a field focused on the technology and principles
 behind how computers communicate and exchange data. It encompasses both the physical aspects of connecting devices
 (like cables and wireless signals) and the logical aspects (like protocols and software) that enable reliable and efficient data 
transfer.
