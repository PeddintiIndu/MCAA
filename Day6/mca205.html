About E-commerce 

E-commerce, or electronic commerce, refers to the buying and selling of goods and services over the internet.
 It encompasses all online activities related to the transaction, including browsing, purchasing, payment, and delivery. 
E-commerce platforms can range from websites and mobile apps to online marketplaces like Amazon and eBay.
