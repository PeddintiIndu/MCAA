About ds lab 

A Data Structures (DS) lab, also sometimes called a Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) lab, is a practical course in 
computer science curricula focused on implementing and experimenting with various data structures and algorithms.
 These labs provide students with hands-on experience in coding, understanding the behavior of algorithms, and analyzing
 their efficiency.
