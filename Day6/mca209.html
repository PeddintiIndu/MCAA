About coor lab 

Operations Research (OR) is a discipline focused on using analytical methods, often mathematical models, 
to improve decision-making in complex systems. It's an interdisciplinary field, drawing from computer science, applied
 mathematics, management, and industrial engineering. The goal is to find optimal or near-optimal solutions to problems by
 analyzing and modeling them using techniques like optimization, simulation, and probability.
