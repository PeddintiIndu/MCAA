About Ds 

"DS" typically refers to DS Group, an Indian multi-business conglomerate, or DS Automobiles,
 a French automotive brand. The DS Group, founded in 1929, is known for its presence in FMCG, hospitality, and other
 sectors. DS Automobiles, established in 2014, focuses on premium vehicles and embodies the French art of travel.
