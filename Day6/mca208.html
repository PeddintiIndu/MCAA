About adbms lab 

ADBMS (Database Management System) lab provides students with hands-on experience in designing, implementing,
 and managing databases. It focuses on practical aspects of database concepts, including data modeling, query languages
 (like SQL), and database administration. Students learn to use various DBMS software, such as Oracle, MySQL, 
or MS Access, to create, manipulate, and retrieve data.
